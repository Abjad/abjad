Future development of duration in Abjad

One additional duration rewrite remains. No duration.absolute attribute currently exists, and it needs to. Also, both _LeafDurationInterface and the various container duration interfaces implement the comparison operators in suboptimal ways; that needs to fix, too. Finally, we need to decide whether to give named-attributed access to the seven different combinations of duration.written, duration.prolated and duration.multiplied taken 1, 2 or 3 at a time, or, alternatively, whether to leave certain of the seven different combinations implicit.

Commentary

1. The duration model predates any of the other major subsystems in Abjad, going back to the first author's model of nested expressions in Mathematica. The Python-based precusors to Abjad &mdash the first author's Lascaux and the second author's Cuepatlahto systems &mdash both included explicit Duration classes as, indeed, did the first versions of Abjad. The decision to remove the explicit Duration class in favor of only Rational instances instead reflected a desire to simplify the core type classification of the system and externalize duration-specific formatting and analysis.
