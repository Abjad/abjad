Example

  abjad> tuplet = FixedMultiplierTuplet((5, 6), Note(0, (1, 16)) * 6)
  abjad> Beam(tuplet)
  abjad> outer = FixedMultiplierTuplet((2, 3), [Note(0, (1, 16)), tuplet])
  abjad> measure = Measure((2, 8), [outer])
  abjad> staff = RhythmicStaff([measure])
  abjad> show(staff)

Definitions

multiplier: positive rational. Prolation factor tuplet donates to its contents. 

  abjad> tuplet.duration.multiplier
  Rational(5, 6)

augmentation: Boolean. True when tuplet multiplier is greater than 1. False otherwise. 

  abjad> tuplet.duration.augmentation
  False

diminution: Boolean. True when tuplet multiplier is less than 1. False otherwise. 

  abjad> tuplet.duration.diminution
  True

prolated: nonnegative rational. See prolated duration. 

  abjad> tuplet.duration.prolated
  Rational(5, 24)

prolation: positive rational. Prolation tuplet receives from its parents, expressed as a single factor. See prolation. 

  abjad> tuplet.duration.prolation
  Rational(2, 3)

prolations: list of positive rationals. Prolation chain tuplet receives from its parents, expressed as a list of factors. See prolation. 

  abjd> tuplet.duration.prolations
  [Rational(2, 3)]
